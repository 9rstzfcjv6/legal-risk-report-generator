MedTech AI Supplier Agreement

This agreement is entered into between BioVision Medical Technologies Ltd. and DataSense AI Solutions Ltd. for the development and supply of AI-assisted diagnostic software components.

The supplier will provide machine learning support services, software integration assistance and technical documentation related to medical imaging analytics.

The parties may exchange confidential technical information, product specifications, prototype documentation, patient-related metadata, anonymized datasets, algorithmic performance reports and research outputs.

The agreement states that each party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property. However, the agreement does not clearly define ownership of foreground IP, jointly developed improvements, model fine-tuning outputs or derivative works created during the collaboration.

The supplier may use technical documentation, software logs, anonymized datasets and performance feedback to improve its internal models and machine learning systems.

The agreement includes confidentiality obligations during the term of the project, but does not specify whether confidentiality obligations survive termination or expiration of the agreement.

The agreement allows the supplier to reference the collaboration in technical materials and case studies, but does not require prior written approval before publication or disclosure of project results.

The supplier must comply with applicable laws and industry standards, but the agreement does not provide a detailed data governance framework for AI training, data retention, access control, audit rights or restrictions on secondary use of technical data.

The project will involve collaboration between legal, R&D, data governance and product teams.
